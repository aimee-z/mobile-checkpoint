Aimee Zheng

Aimee Zheng is a designer who specializes in oil painting and the surreal.

Based in Ann Arbor, Aimee Zheng is a sophomore at the University of Michigan currently pursuing a dual-degree: a BBA through the Ross School of Business and a BSI through the School of Information in the UI/UX track.

In her journey of creating human-centric art, she is naturally drawn to documenting the same central themes in the majority of her paintings, which is to explore the many dualities present in our lives: inside/outside, before/after, and animal/human. Each piece is an amalgamation of these. What is beauty? What is nature? She is curious about the in-between, the journey of it all, and the unusual. Her favorite subject matter and pieces have also always included a sense of the catharsis.

On campus, Aimee is also involved with a student consulting organization called BOND Consulting Group, as well as a professional business fraternity, Phi Beta Lambda, where she operates as the VP of Communications. She has produced multiple pieces of art that have won awards in Scholastic Art Awards and other art competitions, including book publications. Aimee has also pursued her artistic passions in ways that serve her local community, as she independently founded and designed a program in 2018 to teach elementary and middle school students how to create digital art.
